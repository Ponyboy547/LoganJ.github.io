The Fascinating World of Manga and Anime

Manga (漫画) is the Japanese term for comics. Outside of Japan, this term is often used to describe comics that originate from Japan.
Similarly, anime (アニメ) is a Japanese term that refers to cartoons or animated films, but is used outside of Japan to refer specficially to cartoons from Japan.
Both manga and anime used to be fairly niche, but have since exploded in popularity and become well known.
